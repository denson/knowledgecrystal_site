## What Drives My Project Selection

I focus on projects that:

- **Solve Real Problems**: Address actual pain points that teams face when deploying AI
- **Require Reliability**: When hallucinations or errors have real consequences
- **Involve Scale**: Systems that need to work across diverse inputs and use cases
- **Push Innovation**: Explore new approaches to making AI systems more trustworthy
